What it does

Built to close the gap between
good intentions and real impact.

🗺️
Real-Time Need Map

An interactive Leaflet.js map displays shelter locations alongside their current, time-sensitive donation needs — clustered for clarity and updated dynamically as needs change.

🎟️
Request Verification System

Homeless individuals submit specific needs through help centers, which issue collection tickets. Donations are matched to real, verified requests — not guesswork.

🏅
Gamified Engagement

Friends leaderboards, milestone achievement badges, and a community heat map keep donors motivated and celebrate impact — making giving feel meaningful and social.

📋
Donation Tracking Dashboard

Donors can view all their in-progress commitments in one place, with drop-off time windows clearly displayed so nothing slips through the cracks.

How it works

From intention to action
in four simple steps.

01
Choose what to give

Select food, water, or clothing from the category menu on the map screen.

02
See nearby needs

Browse a list of shelters near you sorted by distance, each with specific, real-time requests.

03
Commit to help

Choose how many people you can assist and submit your commitment — you'll get a time window for drop-off.

04
Track your impact

See your donation in-progress, earn badges, and climb the community leaderboard.

Live Demo

Try it right here.

The full app runs in your browser — no install needed. Select a donation category on the map, pick a shelter, commit to helping, and explore your profile and leaderboard.
